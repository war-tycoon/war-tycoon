## 🐛 Common Issues and Fixes

**Script won't execute**
Ensure your executor is fully attached to the Roblox process before running. Restart both applications and re-attempt execution.

**GUI doesn't appear after execution**
Press `RightShift` to toggle the dashboard. Some executors introduce a 3–5 second rendering delay after execution completes.

**Auto farm stops collecting after a few minutes**
This usually occurs after a server teleport resets the script state. Re-execute once you're fully loaded back into your tycoon base.

**Infinite ammo not applying to vehicles**
Open the GUI and ensure the vehicle-specific ammo toggle is enabled under the Combat tab. Some vehicles require a separate toggle from infantry weapons.

**Fly module causing rubber-band rollbacks**
Reduce the fly speed multiplier to 1.5–2x. Very high speeds trigger Roblox's velocity sanity checker, causing rollback events.

**Auto operation skipping missions**
Confirm that the correct operation type is selected in the GUI's operation filter. Mixed operation queues can cause the module to skip incompatible mission types.

**ESP not rendering enemy vehicles**
Vehicle ESP requires a separate toggle from player ESP. Enable "Vehicle ESP" in the Combat tab — it is off by default to reduce rendering load.
